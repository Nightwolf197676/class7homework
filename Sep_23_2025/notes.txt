Linux  basic commands:
cd (change directory)
ls (list files in current directory)
clear (clears the screen)
touch example.txt (creates a new file called example.txt)
pwd (print working directory)
cd .. (moves backwards one directory)
history (lists commands recently ran from the terminal)
mkdir Theo (Creates a directory named Theo. Keep in mind that for Max and Linux it is case sensitive)
